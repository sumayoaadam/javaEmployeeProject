It integrates various functionalities,including recruitment, onboarding, attendance tracking, performance evaluation,
and payroll management. The primary goal of an EMS is to streamline human resource
(HR) processes, improve efficiency, and enhance employee satisfaction.
The recruitment module within an EMS facilitates job postings, candidate tracking,
and resume management.
It allows HR managers to post job vacancies on multiple platforms and track applications
in a centralized database. This module often includes tools for screening resumes,
scheduling interviews, and communicating with candidates.
